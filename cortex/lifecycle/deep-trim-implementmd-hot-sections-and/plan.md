- **`$model` island stays byte-unchanged**: no task touches `implement.md` §2b lines ~173-176 (the `cortex-resolve-model … halt and escalate` block) — that dedup is #353's, and #353 must run sequential-*after* #348 (same file + same mirror under the drift gate).
